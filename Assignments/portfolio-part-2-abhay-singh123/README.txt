Portfolio 2 includes the analysis of the E-Commerce dataset. 

Firstly, I imported the dataset and printed the total length. 

After that, I explored the dataset where I showed the head of the dataset. Then I converted the categorical features to numerical features and displayed the main correlations regarding "rating".

Furthermore, I split the dataset twice, once training data containing 10% of the data and once training data containing 90% of the data.

After that, I trained the Linear Regression models and evaluated the them against both training data sets. Then I visually represented each model and wrote a comprehensive analysis for each. 

Finally I completed an analysis of two figures and wrote about data science ethics.
